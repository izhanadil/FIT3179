Global YouTube Statistics 2023

Top 10 YouTube Channels by Total Subscribers

This visualization showcases the top 10 YouTube channels based on their total number of subscribers. The data has been filtered to include only the channels with the highest ranks. Each bar represents a channel, and the color indicates the category to which the channel belongs. The chart provides a clear comparison of subscriber counts among the top channels.

YouTube Channel Engagement: Subscribers vs. Video Views

This scatter plot visualizes the relationship between the number of subscribers and the total video views for YouTube channels. Each point represents a channel, and its position on the plot indicates the level of engagement in terms of subscribers and video views. Channels are color-coded by category, allowing for easy differentiation. The tooltips provide detailed information about each channel, including its name, subscriber count, video views, and category.

YouTube Channel Subscribers: Category-wise Distribution

This box plot visualization provides a distribution of subscriber counts across different categories of YouTube channels. Each box represents a category, showing the median, interquartile range (IQR), and any potential outliers. The whiskers extend up to 1.5 times the IQR. Categories are color-coded for easy identification. This visualization allows for a quick overview of the spread of subscribers within each category.
